Getting Started

Welcome to the Catering Management app! This tool is designed to be your all-in-one solution for managing clients, creating a library of your dishes, and planning events down to the last detail.

Core Concepts

The app is built around three main components: your Dish Library, your Clients, and your Events. For the best experience, it's recommended to add some dishes and clients before creating your first event.

Feature Guide

Dashboard

Get a quick overview of all events scheduled within the next 7 days.

Event Management

Create, manage, and print detailed event orders, complete with custom menus and financial details.

Client Management

Keep a record of all your clients, including their contact details and address for invoices.

Dish Library

A central repository of all your menu items, including categories and price per serving, for easy access.

Preferences & Data

Manage your data with Auto-Save for safety or use the manual Import/Export tools for portability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this app on multiple devices?
Not simultaneously. The data is stored locally in the browser on a single computer. To move your data, use the Export feature on the old computer and the Import feature on the new one.
What is Auto-Save and why should I use it?
Auto-Save is the recommended way to keep your data safe. After setting it up once, the app will automatically back up your data to a local file every 15 seconds, but only when changes have been made. This protects you from accidental data loss.
What happens if I delete a Client or a Dish?
If you delete a client or a dish, any events that used them will be affected. A deleted client's name will show as "N/A", and a deleted dish will disappear from any event menus it was part of. This action cannot be undone.
